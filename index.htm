INTRODUCTION TO INFOBILLARD MODULES

Running to all Windows version, using a mouse or touch screen INFOBILLARD allows managing, billing and all the activities of members and non-members connected to rentals game.

INFOBILLARD is fully configurable ....

Therefore, INFOBILLARD modules will save you time and money while improving customer service and give you more efficiency and flexibility in your daily operations.

DIFFERENT MODULES

GAME MANAGEMENT

GAME MANAGEMENT Module allows :

The control 1 to 256 games; the reservations and notes management; the addition of comments and notes, with dates of recall; the criteria according to your billing rate; billing individual or group; open billing; An unlimited number of types games; the security and passwords management; the locations management; management deposits; the waiting list (to members) management; the awards management; an unlimited or limited number of players per game and more ...

PROMOTIONS *

PROMOTIONS Module allows :

Change the regular rate gamming or products; create promotions for free time or for increased bonus points; manage effective periods promotions; automatically activate a promotion in a given period (date and time); create promotions to manually enter and more...

MEMBRES *

MEMBRES Module allows : :

Sheet members with photos; taking photos Webcam; Members with identifying bar code; management side members; activation and deactivation a member; type of members with or without discount rates; subscription management; notes on the States; bank of hours; Members history with their games; shipments by mail promotion; promotional mailings via e-mail; print cards with color photos; promotional cards management; member discounts and more...

PRODUCTS *

PRODUCTS Module allows :

The management of an unlimited number of product categories; the management of an unlimited number of products; the possibility of activation or deactivation of a product; the possibility to create type of premium products; printing coupons premium; manage unlimited promotions on products; inventory management; the minimum quantity management; adding % or $ discount; billing to members; billing table (game); the distribution of products when billing; open billing; prevents theft; better control of your profits and more...

RESERVATIONS *

RESERVATIONS Module allows :

Using a calendar, reservation management by day, week or month; create the types of reservation; manage colors depending on the reservation type; make a reservation for a member or non-member; manage note related reservations and more...

COMMUNICATIONS *

COMMUNICATIONS Module allows :

Mail directly by using a Word document or HTML page like an email; mail merging from the members of our base data according InfoBillard filters on members or on the games in a Word document or HTML for email; manage lists and document submitted; check members with incomplete contact information; print address labels and more ....

VIDEO LOTTERY *

VIDEO LOTTERY Module allows :

The creation of devices and their type; entering amount paid per payment per unit; transactions lot for a group of devices; printing reports, printing payments on the X or Z cash report and more ...

SRM * (Quebec only)

The SRM is:

The sales recording module (SRM) is the key device in the technological solution that Revenu Québec has chosen for the restaurant sector.

The SRM InfoBillard module will be compatible with the (SRM photo) Revenue Quebec device.
